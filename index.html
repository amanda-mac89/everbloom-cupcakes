About Everbloom Cupcakes

Hello, I'm Kelly.

I love animals, cupcakes, and collecting beautiful things. When I am not baking, I work full-time in marketing for a radiation oncology school. I currently reside in Dallas, Texas, but my dream is to live somewhere with four full seasons, including snow in the Winter!

I love digging into a good book or spending time with my growing family. I thoroughly enjoy watching re-runs of The Great British Baking Show, The Office, or Gilmore Girls. I hope you enjoy my delicious creations.
